Associate professor at INSA Lyon and the LIRIS lab (French lab for computer science) since September 2025, her research interests lie between heterogeneous data management, interoperability, data exploration, decentralized and federated learning. She usually applies her researches to various domains, such as urban life, journalism and healthcare. She was previously a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Electronics, Information, and Bioengineering at Politecnico di Milano (Italy) from April 2024 to July 2025 working on solutions for healthcare federated analyses from heterogenous sources. From 2021 to 2024, she pursued her PhD at Inria Saclay (France), under the supervision of Pr. Ioana Manolescu, working on integrating, summarizing and exploring heterogeneous datasets. She graduated from University of Lyon (France), where she obtained her CS Master in 2020. She is also the member of several PCs (including DBKDA 2025, ADBIS 2025 and the workshop iSAILS 2025) and has been a reviewer for more than 10 venues.
